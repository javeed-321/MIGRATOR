# Points & coupons request

Administrators of an org can manage goodwill requests and also issue points or coupons directly to customers.

# Goodwill coupons

## Issue Goodwill coupon

To issue a coupon to a customer, perform the following:

1. On the Member Care homepage, search for the customer you want to issue a coupon.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="75% " src="https://files.readme.io/dd7bf323bf5118794df4dd8aed54ce3666b6c9fb781c5f88f08d6329ec822310-image.png" className="border" />

2. Select the ellipsis menu on the Customer Single View (CSV) page, select **Goodwill request**, and then from the dropdown, select **Coupons**.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="75% " src="https://files.readme.io/d491904ff4bc14a54e42cd26ca16b3377ceafdc7a6352771116ff321e5ffcfcc-image.png" className="border" />

3. From the **Select coupon** dropdown, choose any coupon of your choice.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/21a2913c61df96698e70525217e436ae9c944a6a2f3ebfffdace508ada3fe0bc-image.png" className="border" />

4. From the **Reason** dropdown, select the reason for issuing goodwill coupons.\
   **Note**: The reasons available in this dropdown are configured in the org's **Goodwill Adjustment Settings**. To add or manage reasons, refer to <Anchor label="Goodwill Coupons Adjustment Settings" target="_blank" href="https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/issue-goodwill-points-coupons#goodwill-coupon-adjustment-settings">Goodwill Coupons Adjustment Settings</Anchor>.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/e0627f03965b9ab2c705461c44e5de77ef751609954ad1d3520c48bfdcabf489-image.png" className="border" />

5. In the **Comments** section, enter a note for issuing the goodwill coupon.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/bf46d12bd6a7ab53ecfb70e5dfd4910c14b06cfc8350c9f337e5dc3d2e0c9617-image.png" className="border" />

6. Select **Send request**.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="35% " src="https://files.readme.io/bed259c732b12ffa6f824958f0949f76d7acf547995aed228f31518cc30802b9-image.png" className="border" />

A coupon is issued to the customer.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/f9e36d4c199f0f8970b9a10727f93defdab714564621bbbdb98d718a8fcb517c-Screenshot_2026-05-14_at_3.50.58_PM.png" className="border" />

To approve goodwill coupon requests, refer to the old Member Care documentation [here](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/issue-goodwill-points-coupons).

## Issue Goodwill points

> 📘 Note:
>
> By default, auto approve is enabled for points issual.
>
> To successfully credit goodwill points, you must ensure that the Points Adjustment Settings toggle is ON and a Default Till is configured. If either setting is missing, the system will approve the request but will not credit points to the customer.

To issue goodwill points to a customer, perform the following:

1. On the Member Care homepage, search for the customer you want to issue points.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="75% " src="https://files.readme.io/dd7bf323bf5118794df4dd8aed54ce3666b6c9fb781c5f88f08d6329ec822310-image.png" className="border" />

2. Select the ellipsis menu on the Customer Single View (CSV) page, select **Goodwill request**, and then from the dropdown, select **Points**.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="75% " src="https://files.readme.io/a575ad9b1a32435671f5fc764b861e69d1fbf349dfe1a0fb010c8d28f94e0cff-image.png" className="border" />

3. Enter the points you want to add in the **Points to be added** field.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/f9b28f12ba34446eb4bc27b8cfb9189888d3746d808d0cee8cca97bba53d3420-image.png" className="border" />

4. From the **Program** dropdown, choose the appropriate program.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/b83592f9d4a817a6478a41fbc0695a13672e9ed4ca78a1f15810c9caf6d55038-image.png" className="border" />

5. From the **Reason** dropdown, select the reason for issuing goodwill points.\
   **Note**: The reasons available in this dropdown are configured in the org's **Goodwill Adjustment Settings**. To add or manage reasons, refer to <Anchor label="Goodwill Points Adjustment Settings" target="_blank" href="https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/issue-goodwill-points-coupons#goodwill-points-adjustment-settings">Goodwill Points Adjustment Settings</Anchor>.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/d49cf28e0a76e5445dd4f92c0cd401d628bbf8aac46134fac137a5a1b29d4b80-image.png" className="border" />

8. In the **Comments** section, enter a note for issuing the goodwill coupon.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="40% " src="https://files.readme.io/df2c842ea50695aebc46ccd64d8165e05981691b2df20f7b8c61780d1f5b6f19-image.png" className="border" />

8. Click **Save**.

<Image align="center" border={true} width="30% " src="https://files.readme.io/0f464d9e79c497585181c8d12eb5e5a0b4277ee9d33162d9ad28e5ffb8f85867-image.png" className="border" />

Points have been issued to the customer.
