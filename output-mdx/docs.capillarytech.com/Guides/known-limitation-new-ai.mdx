## Known Limitations

Below are the known limitations of the Aira-coder, which will be addressed in future releases:

* Token size limitations may restrict complex dataflows.
* Nesting or calling one tag from another is not yet supported.
* No support for external libraries as of now.
* You must use the Send icon to submit a prompt. Pressing the Enter key does not send the prompt.
