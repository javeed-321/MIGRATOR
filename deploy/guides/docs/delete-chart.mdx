## Delete Chart

To delete a chart, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to **Library** > **Charts**.
2. **Select** the chart type - **Normal**, **Migration** or **Funnel**.
3. In **Search Charts**, search the chart to delete either from the list or search by the name.

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/8ca6bfa-Locate_chart.png" width="1920" height="1080" alt="" />

4. Click **Delete**.

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/b37e041-Delete_Chart.png" width="1920" height="1080" alt="" />

5. Confirm **Yes** to delete the chart.

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/21e5aaa-Delete_Confirm.png" width="1920" height="1080" alt="" />
